Vexa Lite deployed.

This chart deploys a SINGLE container (vexa-lite) and sets:
- ORCHESTRATOR=process (no Docker socket; bots are spawned as child processes)

Service:
  {{ include "vexa-lite.fullname" . }}:{{ .Values.service.port }}

To port-forward:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "vexa-lite.fullname" . }} 8056:{{ .Values.service.port }}

Then use API_HOST = http://localhost:8056
